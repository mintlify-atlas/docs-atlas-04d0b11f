## Understanding Quotas

All plans enforce monthly quotas to ensure fair usage. When you reach your monthly event limit, you'll need to upgrade to continue sending events. Learn more about [how quotas work](/pricing/quotas).

## Upgrading Your Plan

Upgrading is instant and processed securely through Stripe. Your new quota limits take effect immediately after payment is confirmed.
